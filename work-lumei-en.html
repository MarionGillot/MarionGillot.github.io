lumei

My challenge was to help develop a more appropriable and distinctive brand that would reflect the values values of the organisation.

The brand

Lumei aims to offer elegant and timeless clothing that can be worn for several seasons. The collections are designed to be easy to wear and combine, while offering modern cuts and careful details. The brand's minimalist aesthetic allows the quality of materials and finishes to shine through, while offering a subtle and elegant colour palette. subtle and elegant colour palette.

The logo

The logo has an elegant and soft typography in its corners. Its letters are black on a transparent background, allowing them to blend in with any background on the site or other broadcasting platforms

Lumei is a name that evokes light and beauty, yet is easy to pronounce and remember. The name reflects the brand's elegant and timeless aesthetic, while evoking values of sustainability and ethics.

Lumei offers an online and in-store shopping experience that focuses on customer service, transparency and accessibility. Customers can browse the collections online, while enjoying fast delivery and a flexible return policy.

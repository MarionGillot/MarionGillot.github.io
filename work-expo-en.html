World Expo 2025 poster

The objective of an Expo is to bring together actors from all over the world to find solutions to a fundamental challenge of humanity. This challenge is the theme of the Expo. At the World Expo 2020 Dubai, the theme will be "Connecting Minds, Building the Future»

The concept

The concept expressed in the poster is the future city living in harmony with the environment, the well-being of nature and animals, saving lives.

This concept is expressed through the greenery mixed with the sea, the sky, and the living beings being able to live in harmony with man in his ecological and environmentally friendly city. man in his ecological and environmentally friendly city.

Themes :

  • Connecting lives
  • Inspiring lives
  • Saving lives

The logo

Focusing on the official logo of the 2025 exhibition, I could see that it was cells, with red and blue colours. So I went further down the medical path, and chose DNA: a raised shape, known to all

Two versions are proposed for different formats, more or less dark. Here, it is the logo with lighter and brighter colours Here, the logo with lighter and brighter colours is used for the poster of the exhibition.

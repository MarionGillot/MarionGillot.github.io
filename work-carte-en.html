Postcard of Cassandre

Creation of a postcard on the theme of Cassandre alias, Adolphe Jean-Marie Mouron, using his graphic aspects and his drawing methods. Inspired work: TONIC WINE DUBONNET WITH QUIQUENA

The objective

The objective of this project was to create a postcard based on the methods and techniques used by the artist Cassandre. Cassandre aka, Adolphe Jean-Marie Mouron, who is a French graphic designer, typographer, poster artist, theatre decorator, lithographer and painter of the interwar period.

By hijacking the subject of the poster 'Dubonnet VIN TONIQUE DUBONNET WITH QUIQUENA' with Adobe illustrator, I then put this content in black to highlight it: In contrast with the background of the postcard, representing the beach, in Spain. This would be a postcard for holiday makers, those who visit the city, those who love the artist.

Inspired by the work Dubonnet VIN TONIQUE DUBONNET WITH QUIQUENA

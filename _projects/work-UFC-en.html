About

Over the past six centuries, the University of Franche-Comté has been a bastion of academic excellence, a guardian of heritage and tradition, a catalyst for innovation and modernity, a defender of open-mindedness and diversity, and a pioneer in its commitment to environmental sustainability.

Logo

All elements of the logo are and must be aligned evenly. Evenness is a signature feature of the logo and refers to one of the university's values. These spacing guidelines apply to the 100px by 50px size, which is the minimum size for the logo.

The concept

The choice of the album "Gamze Aydemir" was motivated by the desire to create a cover for a timeless music project, which has a melancholic but also romantic atmosphere. Gamze Aydemir's songs are often soft and full of emotion, which inspired the creation of a cover that evokes these feelings.

The cover was designed in black and white to reflect the nostalgic and romantic mood of the album. The traces of water flowing on the right side of the cover evoke the idea of fluidity and the passing of time. The image of a woman in The image of a woman in black and white within the water marks adds a poetic and enigmatic dimension to the cover...

The image of the woman was chosen to represent the poetry and sweetness of the album, and was worked in black and white to create a classic and elegant effect. and white to create a classic and elegant effect.

The album title "Gamze Aydemir" is written in white capital letters, contrasting with the black background of the cover. The text "Just friends." is written in black at the top of the cover, creating a visual balance and adding an element of mystery to the cover. to the cover.

The objective

The aim was to create a clear, striking and attractive visual that would enable visitors to quickly understand:

    • Key figures for the company (performance, highlights, results)

    • The activities and services offered to customers, illustrating the company's added value

    • Geographical presence and locations, to highlight its influence and development

The design was conceived to combine readability and visual impact, with a hierarchy of information that guides the viewer's gaze. The result is a professional, effective communication tool that reflects the company's identity.

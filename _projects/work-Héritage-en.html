The concept

The book will consist of images of Monet's works, with some explanatory texts. However, another exciting feature is included: with a VR headset, you will be able to see a visual representation of the artist painting the work in question. All you need to do is scan the image of the chosen work with your phone. If you do not have a VR headset, a version will also be available on your phone thanks to an app. All you have to do is scan the image of the chosen work with your phone. If you don't have a VR headset, a version will also be available on your phone via an app. The visual representation will be animated and in 360° for an even more realistic experience, offering a trip back in time!

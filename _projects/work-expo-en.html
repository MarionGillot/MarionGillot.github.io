The concept

The concept expressed in the poster is the future city living in harmony with the environment, the well-being of nature and animals, saving lives.

This concept is expressed through the greenery mixed with the sea, the sky, and the living beings being able to live in harmony with man in his ecological and environmentally friendly city. man in his ecological and environmentally friendly city.

Themes :

  • Inspiring lives
  • Saving lives
